# Individual Customers

**Stakeholder Type**: Primary Stakeholder

## Overview

Individual customers represent the core user base of the car rental system. They are private individuals who rent vehicles for personal use, including leisure travel, business trips, temporary transportation needs, or special occasions. This stakeholder group encompasses a wide spectrum from budget-conscious travelers to premium luxury seekers.

## Stakeholder Profile

### Demographics
- **Age Range**: 21-75 years (with peak usage 25-55)
- **Income Levels**: Varies from budget-conscious to high-net-worth individuals
- **Travel Frequency**: Occasional renters to frequent travelers
- **Tech Savviness**: Ranges from basic smartphone users to tech-early adopters
- **Geographic Distribution**: Urban, suburban, and rural locations

### User Segments
1. **Leisure Travelers**: Vacation rentals, weekend getaways, road trips
2. **Business Travelers**: Work-related trips, client meetings, conferences
3. **Temporary Need Users**: Vehicle in repair, testing before purchase, special events
4. **Premium Seekers**: Luxury vehicle experiences, special occasions, status-conscious
5. **Budget Travelers**: Price-sensitive, seeking basic reliable transportation

## Goals

### Primary Goals
1. **Find Suitable Vehicle**: Quickly locate a vehicle that meets specific needs (size, features, price)
2. **Transparent Pricing**: Understand total cost upfront with no hidden fees
3. **Seamless Booking**: Complete reservation process quickly and easily
4. **Reliable Service**: Receive a clean, well-maintained vehicle as described
5. **Flexible Options**: Ability to modify or cancel bookings when plans change
6. **Convenient Pickup/Return**: Minimal wait times and straightforward processes
7. **Safe Transaction**: Secure payment processing and data protection
8. **Fair Treatment**: Clear policies, reasonable damage assessments, responsive support

### Secondary Goals
1. **Loyalty Rewards**: Earn benefits for repeat business
2. **Personalized Experience**: Saved preferences, tailored recommendations
3. **Multi-Channel Access**: Book via web, mobile app, or phone
4. **Insurance Clarity**: Understand coverage options and make informed decisions
5. **Location Convenience**: Access to multiple pickup/return locations
6. **Vehicle Variety**: Wide selection across price points and vehicle types

## Pain Points

### Booking & Search Pain Points
1. **Hidden Fees**: Surprise charges at pickup (insurance, fuel, taxes, airport fees)
2. **Unclear Availability**: Vehicles shown as available but not actually in stock
3. **Complex Pricing**: Difficulty comparing total costs across providers
4. **Limited Filters**: Cannot search by specific features (e.g., USB ports, trunk size)
5. **Misleading Photos**: Vehicle condition doesn't match online images
6. **Confusing Categories**: ACRISS codes and vehicle classes not intuitive

### Booking Process Pain Points
1. **Lengthy Forms**: Too many required fields during checkout
2. **Account Requirements**: Forced registration before viewing prices
3. **Payment Friction**: Limited payment methods, declined cards
4. **Mobile Difficulties**: Poor mobile experience, hard to complete on phone
5. **Modification Complexity**: Difficult to change dates or vehicle type
6. **Cancellation Penalties**: Unclear or excessive cancellation fees

### Pickup & Return Pain Points
1. **Long Wait Times**: Queues at rental counter, slow processing
2. **Pressure Tactics**: Aggressive upselling of insurance and upgrades
3. **Vehicle Condition Issues**: Dirty vehicles, mechanical problems, fuel discrepancies
4. **Damage Disputes**: Unfair charges for pre-existing damage
5. **Unclear Instructions**: Confusing return procedures, after-hours drop-off
6. **Documentation Burden**: Excessive paperwork, redundant ID checks

### Service & Support Pain Points
1. **Unresponsive Support**: Difficulty reaching customer service
2. **Roadside Assistance Delays**: Long wait times for breakdowns
3. **Billing Errors**: Incorrect charges, delayed refunds
4. **Communication Gaps**: Lack of booking confirmations, reminders
5. **Language Barriers**: Limited language support for international travelers
6. **Dispute Resolution**: Slow or unfair handling of complaints

### Premium User Specific Pain Points
1. **Limited Luxury Selection**: Insufficient high-end vehicle options
2. **Inconsistent Quality**: Luxury vehicles not maintained to expected standards
3. **No Concierge Service**: Lack of white-glove treatment
4. **Generic Experience**: No personalization or VIP recognition
5. **Privacy Concerns**: Data sharing, marketing communications

### Budget User Specific Pain Points
1. **Price Creep**: Base price low but total cost inflated by mandatory add-ons
2. **Limited Availability**: Budget vehicles often sold out
3. **Older Vehicles**: Economy cars in poor condition
4. **Inflexible Policies**: Strict cancellation, no grace periods
5. **Nickel-and-Diming**: Charges for every small item (GPS, child seat, additional driver)

## Key Interactions with the System

### Pre-Booking Phase
1. **Vehicle Search**: Browse available vehicles by location, dates, and filters
2. **Price Comparison**: Compare rates across vehicle types and rental periods
3. **Vehicle Details Review**: View photos, specifications, features, and reviews
4. **Location Selection**: Choose pickup and return locations
5. **Account Creation**: Register or sign in (optional for browsing)

### Booking Phase
1. **Date Selection**: Specify pickup and return dates/times
2. **Vehicle Selection**: Choose specific vehicle or vehicle class
3. **Add-Ons Selection**: Choose insurance, GPS, child seats, additional drivers
4. **Driver Information**: Provide license details and contact information
5. **Payment Processing**: Enter payment details and complete transaction
6. **Confirmation Receipt**: Receive booking confirmation via email/SMS

### Pre-Pickup Phase
1. **Booking Management**: View, modify, or cancel reservation
2. **Reminder Notifications**: Receive pickup reminders and instructions
3. **Digital Check-In**: Complete pre-arrival paperwork (if available)
4. **Location Directions**: Access pickup location details and directions

### Pickup Phase
1. **Counter Check-In**: Present ID and confirmation at rental desk
2. **Contract Signing**: Review and sign rental agreement
3. **Vehicle Inspection**: Walk-around inspection with staff
4. **Key Collection**: Receive keys and parking location
5. **Departure**: Exit facility with vehicle

### During Rental Phase
1. **Roadside Assistance**: Contact support for breakdowns or issues
2. **Extension Requests**: Request rental period extension
3. **Support Inquiries**: Ask questions or report problems
4. **Fuel Management**: Locate fuel stations, understand fuel policy

### Return Phase
1. **Return Location**: Navigate to return facility
2. **Vehicle Inspection**: Staff inspection for damage
3. **Fuel Verification**: Confirm fuel level meets policy
4. **Key Return**: Hand over keys and complete return
5. **Receipt Collection**: Receive final invoice and charges

### Post-Rental Phase
1. **Invoice Review**: Check final charges and billing
2. **Dispute Filing**: Contest incorrect charges or damage claims
3. **Feedback Submission**: Rate experience and provide reviews
4. **Loyalty Points**: View earned rewards and benefits
5. **Rebooking**: Make future reservations based on positive experience

## Success Metrics

### Satisfaction Metrics
1. **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Likelihood to recommend service (target: >50)
2. **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)**: Overall satisfaction rating (target: >4.5/5)
3. **Review Ratings**: Average rating on review platforms (target: >4.3/5)
4. **Repeat Booking Rate**: Percentage of customers who rebook (target: >40%)
5. **Referral Rate**: Customers who refer friends/family (target: >25%)

### Efficiency Metrics
1. **Booking Completion Time**: Average time to complete reservation (target: &lt;5 minutes)
2. **Search to Booking Conversion**: Percentage who book after searching (target: >15%)
3. **Mobile Booking Rate**: Percentage of bookings via mobile (target: >60%)
4. **Pickup Wait Time**: Average wait at counter (target: &lt;10 minutes)
5. **Return Processing Time**: Time to complete return (target: &lt;5 minutes)

### Quality Metrics
1. **Vehicle Condition Score**: Customer rating of vehicle cleanliness/condition (target: >4.7/5)
2. **Accuracy Rate**: Vehicle matches description (target: >98%)
3. **Breakdown Rate**: Mechanical issues during rental (target: &lt;0.5%)
4. **Damage Dispute Rate**: Contested damage charges (target: &lt;2%)
5. **Billing Accuracy**: Correct charges on first invoice (target: >97%)

### Support Metrics
1. **First Response Time**: Time to initial support response (target: &lt;2 hours)
2. **Resolution Time**: Average time to resolve issues (target: &lt;24 hours)
3. **Support Satisfaction**: Rating of support interactions (target: >4.5/5)
4. **Self-Service Rate**: Issues resolved without agent contact (target: >70%)
5. **Escalation Rate**: Issues requiring management intervention (target: &lt;5%)

### Financial Metrics
1. **Average Booking Value**: Revenue per reservation (track trend)
2. **Upsell Acceptance Rate**: Percentage accepting add-ons (target: >30%)
3. **Refund Rate**: Percentage of bookings refunded (target: &lt;5%)
4. **Chargeback Rate**: Payment disputes (target: &lt;0.5%)
5. **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Total revenue per customer over lifetime (track trend)

### Engagement Metrics
1. **Account Creation Rate**: Percentage who create accounts (target: >50%)
2. **App Download Rate**: Mobile app installations (target: >40% of mobile users)
3. **Loyalty Program Enrollment**: Percentage joining rewards program (target: >60%)
4. **Email Open Rate**: Engagement with communications (target: >25%)
5. **Modification Rate**: Bookings changed before pickup (track for flexibility needs)

## Relationship to Features

### Critical Features for Individual Customers
1. **Advanced Search & Filtering**: Find vehicles by price, type, features, location
2. **Transparent Pricing**: Clear breakdown of all costs before booking
3. **Mobile-Optimized Booking**: Seamless mobile web and app experience
4. **Flexible Modification**: Easy date changes, vehicle swaps, cancellations
5. **Digital Check-In**: Skip counter lines with pre-arrival processing
6. **Real-Time Availability**: Accurate vehicle availability information
7. **Multiple Payment Options**: Credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets
8. **Instant Confirmation**: Immediate booking confirmation via email/SMS
9. **Booking Management Dashboard**: View and manage all reservations
10. **Customer Reviews**: Read experiences from other renters

### Important Features for Premium Segment
1. **Luxury Vehicle Selection**: High-end cars with detailed specifications
2. **Concierge Service**: Dedicated support line, priority assistance
3. **Vehicle Delivery**: Bring car to customer location
4. **Personalized Recommendations**: AI-driven suggestions based on preferences
5. **VIP Loyalty Tiers**: Enhanced benefits for frequent renters
6. **Privacy Controls**: Opt-out of marketing, data sharing preferences

### Important Features for Budget Segment
1. **Price Comparison Tools**: Easy comparison across dates and locations
2. **Discount Codes**: Promotional offers and coupon application
3. **Basic Vehicle Options**: Reliable economy cars at competitive prices
4. **Flexible Cancellation**: Free cancellation windows
5. **Transparent Add-On Pricing**: Clear optional vs mandatory charges
6. **No Hidden Fees**: All-inclusive pricing display
