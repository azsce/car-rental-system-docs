# Deep Search Prompt: Car Rental Platform Features Discovery

## Context & Objective

I need you to conduct a comprehensive deep search to identify **all possible features, innovations, and capabilities** for a modern car rental platform. This search should uncover both established best practices and cutting-edge innovations across the entire car rental ecosystem.

## Search Scope & Perspectives

### 1. Business Models to Explore
- Traditional B2C car rental (airport, city locations)
- B2B corporate rental programs
- Peer-to-peer car sharing (Turo, Getaround model)
- Subscription-based car access
- Fractional ownership programs
- Luxury/exotic car rental
- Long-term lease alternatives
- Ride-sharing integration models

### 2. User Segments to Consider
- **Premium/Luxury Users**: High-net-worth individuals seeking white-glove service, exotic vehicles, concierge features
- **Budget-Conscious Users**: Price-sensitive customers needing transparency, value, and flexibility
- **Business Travelers**: Corporate accounts, expense management, loyalty programs
- **Tourists**: International travelers, multi-city bookings, language support
- **Local Residents**: Weekend rentals, moving assistance, special occasions
- **Young Drivers**: First-time renters, insurance options, education
- **Families**: Child seats, spacious vehicles, safety features
- **Eco-Conscious**: EV rentals, carbon offset programs, sustainability metrics

- Search for: Progressive web apps, mobile-first design, voice interfaces, AR/VR showrooms, chatbot assistants, biometric authentication, offline capabilities, accessibility features

- Search for: Microservices architectures, serverless computing, real-time inventory management, dynamic pricing algorithms, fraud detection systems, blockchain for contracts, IoT vehicle integration

#### Mobile & IoT
- Search for: Keyless entry via smartphone, remote vehicle control, in-car apps, telematics integration, GPS tracking, vehicle health monitoring, contactless pickup/return

#### AI & Machine Learning
- Search for: Predictive maintenance, demand forecasting, personalized recommendations, dynamic pricing optimization, fraud detection, customer service automation, image recognition for damage assessment

#### Payment & Financial
